Our Major Camps

Nau Sainik Camp (NSC)

This camp provides cadets with an intensive experience of naval operations, fostering skills and leadership at sea.

Ship Attachment Camp

Get real-time experience aboard naval vessels, understanding the daily routines and technical operations of the Navy.

Republic Day Camp (RDC)

Be part of the grand Republic Day celebrations, representing your state and NCC unit in one of the most prestigious camps.

Annual Training Camp (ATC)

This camp focuses on the overall training of cadets, building camaraderie and boosting morale through intensive drills and activities.

Sailing Camp

The Sailing Camp in Naval NCC offers cadets hands-on experience in navigating and operating sailboats, enhancing their maritime skills. Cadets learn teamwork, discipline, and the art of sailing through practical sessions on water.

Special Yatching Camp

The Special Yachting Camp in Naval NCC provides cadets with advanced training in sailing and yacht handling. It sharpens their navigation skills while fostering leadership and teamwork in challenging maritime environments.

Ocean Sailing Camp

The Ocean Sailing Camp in Naval NCC immerses cadets in long-distance sailing, honing their seamanship and navigation skills on open waters. It offers a unique experience of handling ocean-going vessels in real maritime conditions

Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat (EBSB) Camp

The Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat (EBSB) Camp in Naval NCC promotes national integration by bringing cadets from different states together. It fosters cultural exchange and unity while encouraging teamwork and mutual understanding among participants.
