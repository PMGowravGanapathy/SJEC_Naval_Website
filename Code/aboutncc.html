The Naval NCC Wing at St. Joseph Engineering College (SJEC) [7/8 SUBUNIT - 6 KAR NAVAL NCC Udupi] is a vibrant and dynamic extension of the National Cadet Corps that is committed to nurturing young minds to become responsible, disciplined, and patriotic citizens. The Naval wing, a cornerstone of SJEC’s extracurricular offerings, provides cadets with a platform to develop leadership qualities, physical fitness, and a strong sense of duty through military-oriented training and life-skill development.

Our Mission

Our mission is to instill a sense of duty towards the nation and its defense forces by training cadets in various naval operations. The SJEC Naval NCC Wing offers rigorous training programs that emphasize naval disciplines, personal character development, and community service. Through participation in this wing, students are encouraged to embody the core values of courage, commitment, and service to the nation.

Training and Activities

Cadets at SJEC undergo comprehensive training in seamanship, navigation, ship modeling, and naval communications. Specialized training in sailing, boating, and scuba diving gives cadets hands-on experience in maritime skills, which sets the Naval Wing apart. Cadets are also trained in drill, physical fitness, weapon handling, and first aid, helping them stay fit and ready for any challenge. These trainings are designed not only to prepare cadets for a potential career in the armed forces but also to build essential life skills like resilience, leadership, and teamwork.

  • Nau Sainik Camp (NSC)
  • Ship Attachment Camp
  • Republic Day Camp (RDC)
  • Annual Training Camps (ATC)

Leadership and Personal Development

Beyond the physical and technical skills, SJEC Naval NCC Wing focuses heavily on developing leadership qualities and team spirit. Each cadet is encouraged to take up roles of responsibility, and through various leadership challenges, they learn to make quick decisions, manage teams, and handle stressful situations effectively. These qualities not only help cadets in NCC but also in their personal and professional lives, shaping them into well-rounded individuals ready to face future challenges.

Community Engagement

The SJEC Naval NCC Wing is also deeply committed to giving back to society. Our cadets engage in community service programs, such as cleanliness drives, environmental awareness campaigns, and disaster relief efforts, contributing positively to the local and national community. This community service instills a sense of social responsibility and humanitarian values in every cadet.

A Gateway to Opportunities

Being part of the Naval NCC Wing opens up numerous opportunities for cadets. Many go on to secure scholarships, attend international exchange programs, or even join the prestigious Indian Armed Forces. The B and C certificates awarded to cadets are highly valued and can serve as stepping stones for a career in defense services, giving students an edge in various competitive exams like the Combined Defense Services (CDS) and SSB interviews.

At SJEC, the Naval NCC Wing is more than just a training program; it is a journey of self-discovery, adventure, and growth. Cadets walk away with lasting friendships, a deep sense of patriotism, and the skills to lead with integrity and honor in all walks of life.

Join us and sail toward a future of courage, leadership, and service!
