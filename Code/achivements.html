National Camps

Cadet Captain Prashanth P Koti represented Karnataka and Goa DTE at Republic Day Camp 2023. He won a Gold Medal in Powered Ship Model and VIP Ship Model in Ship Modeling. He was also awarded the Admiral Nanda Trophy, also known as the Silver Ship, for the first time in the history of Karnataka and Goa DTE.


Cadet Captain Sanath Krishna represented Karnataka and Goa DTE at Republic Day Camp 2025 as Best Cadet - Navy(SD) from Karnataka and Goa directorate in DG NCC CAMP at Cariappa Ground, Delhi from 31 Dec 2024 to 31 Jan 2025.


PO Cadet Lisha D S represented Karnataka and Goa DTE at Republic Day Camp 2025 as Best Cadet - Navy(SW) from Karnataka and Goa directorate in DG NCC CAMP at Cariappa Ground, Delhi from 31 Dec 2024 to 31 Jan 2025. as She got a Best Briefer award for Flag area by Chief of the Army staff,General Upendra Dwivedi.


PO Cadet Tanuksha Raj represented Karnataka and Goa DTE at All India Nau-Sainik Camp 2024.The camp was held at INS Shivaji, Lonavala from August 25 to September 5. Tanushka Raj was part of the boat pulling team of Karnataka & Goa directorate team.


PO Cadet Nandeesh attended Ship Attachment Campin Mumbai. He was trained in receiving instruction in Semaphore, Weapon Handling, and other related areas.
